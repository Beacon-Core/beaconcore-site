Arbiter

Structural comparison layer of BeaconCore.
Stateless. Neutral. Non‑interpretive.

No bias. No semantics. Only structural selection.

Purpose

Arbiter compares sealed outputs and selects the structurally valid vector.
It does not generate, interpret, evaluate, or modify state.

Selection is deterministic and non‑volitional.

Comparison Surface

Inputs are compared across invariant dimensions.
Consistency determines selection.

  • Lineage consistency
  • Invariant compatibility
  • Drift differential
  • State equivalence

Resolution

Resolution produces a single valid vector.
Non‑compliant states collapse.

  • Vector selection
  • Conflict collapse
  • Outcome stabilization
  • Structural stillness
